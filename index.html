The Old-Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 1 sugar cube
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 2 ounces rye whisky
  • club soda

Place the sugar cube in a Old-Fashioned glass. Add bitters and a little club soda. Muddle. Add one large ice cube, followed by the rye.
